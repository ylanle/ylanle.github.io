About Me

I am Ylan Le, a third year undergraduate student at Babson College. Today I am building this website to share my fascination with colored gemstones and the stories behind rare jewels from around the world. I want to explore the history, symbolism, and beauty of unique stones while creating a luxurious and elegant online experience.

My Interests

  • Rare sapphires, emeralds, and rubies
  • The history behind royal gemstone collections
  • Crystal colors and natural formations
  • The symbolism and meaning of precious stones
  • Luxury jewelry and gemstone craftsmanship
